Miscellaneous

I participated the "Convex Presentations of Translation Surfaces" project in LoG(M) in Winter 2021. Here is a write up.

We implemented the Lelievre-Weiss strict convexity test in SageMath.

I also implemented an enumeration of square-tiled surfaces in H(4) in SageMath.

I participated in the "Graphs Associated with Groups" project mentored by Professor Mark Lewis in Kent State REU in Summer 2020.

We studied properties of commuting graphs of certain groups and the structure of solvable tidy groups.

Here is our paper "Extending Morgan and Parker's results about commuting graphs" [arXiv].

I participated in the "Solvability of Multiple Unicast Networks" project mentored by Professor Felice Manganiello in Clemson PREU in Summer 2019.

We studied solvability conditions and algorithms for multiple unicast networks over finite fields.

Here is the abstract for our presentation at the 2020 JMM.
