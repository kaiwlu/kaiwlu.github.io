Zpordle

p-adic Distance

Rules

Each game, a random integer will be picked between 0 and "1000. You will then have 10 guesses to guess the number. Each guess will be associated with a prime number, given in increasing order. For a guess associated with prime p, the game will tell you the largest power of p dividing |target - guess|. Good luck!
