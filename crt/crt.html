Chinese Remainder Theorem

Rules

For each game, a random integer is picked between 0 and 2000. You have 6 guesses to guess the number. Each guess will be associated with a prime number, given in increasing order. For a guess associated with prime p, the game will tell you the remainder of the target number minus your guess mod p. Good luck!
